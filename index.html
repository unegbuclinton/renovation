A More Accessible Home for Our Community

Our masjid is the heartbeat of our community, it’s where we come together for support, education, empowerment and most importantly, to worship Allah (swt).

These renovations will provide even more space, renewed facilities and increased accessibility so that we can welcome even more members of our community to join us. As part of our efforts to make the masjid more accessible to all, we are building an elevator to help our disabled youth easily access the mosque hassle-free.

Your donations will not only help fund these renovations, but will also bring change that will last for generations.
